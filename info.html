Analytical solutions of the advection-dispersion equation

This visualizer provides four types of analytical solutions to the advection-dispersion equation and they are the followings:

1D Continuous Injection
2D Continuous Injection
1D Slug Injection
2D Slug Injection

In addition to the analytical solution, user can change values using slider to study groundwater contamination under various subsurface conditions such as retardation and first-order decay.
